AI In the Agency.

This is not a training program. It is an invitation to help build what this agency becomes next and the resources to do it with.

Where we are going

AI is already here.

The question is no longer whether to use it. It is whether we use it well, with intention, and in a way that makes our work and our people stronger rather than more interchangeable.

This is DCP's plan for doing that. Not a mandate from the top down. A genuine program built around the belief that the people in this building are what makes any of this work in the first place.

Three things are happening in parallel: a conversation series designed to build real skill and provoke real thinking, a certification that gives everyone a shared foundation, and a clear map of what tools are available and how to access them.

This is not something being done to the agency. It is something being built with the people in it. What comes next depends on how much you choose to engage with it.

The Conversation Series

Seven sessions. Real mastery.

Bi-weekly across both agencies, starting [Week 2 date]. Each session is a different format panel, debate, showcase, live build. All of them are designed to build real mastery of a new technology not technical fluency, but the deeper skill of knowing how to think with it, when to trust it, and when to push back.

Week 2

The future must be about creativity
What is your relationship with AI right now? For most people it starts as a search engine and stays there. This session is about changing that: making AI a genuine thought partner that augments your most powerful asset.

Format: Cross-disciplinary panel. The room includes ECDs, senior strategists, and account leads. The central question is not what AI does to creativity but what creativity looks like when intelligence is abundant and the scarce thing is perspective, taste, and point of view. The foundation

DCP CERTIFICATION.

This is not a prompting course. The certification is about building real mastery of a new technology not technical fluency, but the deeper skill of knowing how to think with it, when to trust it, and when to push back. Where AI genuinely sharpens your thinking and where it quietly flattens it. How to use it in a way that builds your instincts rather than outsourcing them.

A significant part of the course covers generative AI guardrails and safety responsible use, data privacy, what not to put into these systems, and how to protect client confidentiality and your own work. These are not optional considerations. They are the foundation everything else is built on.

It's self-paced, self-directed, and yours to complete on your own time. There's a quiz, and a certification at the end that belongs to you regardless of where your career goes.

It's mandatory for both agencies not because we want to check a box, but because we want everyone in these buildings to be in the same conversation from the same foundation. The sessions make more sense when you've done the course. The course makes more sense when you've been in the sessions.
